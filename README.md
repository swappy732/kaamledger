## 🚨 The Problem

India has **400M+ informal workers** — plumbers, electricians, painters, daily wage earners.

They work. They earn. They hustle.

But when it comes to proof?

* ❌ No work history
* ❌ No credibility
* ❌ No access to loans
* ❌ No way to justify higher rates

> Skilled workers remain **invisible to the formal economy**.

---

## 💡 The Solution

**KaamLedger = Proof of Work Infrastructure**

A system where every completed job becomes:

* ✅ Verified
* 🕒 Timestamped
* 💰 Payment recorded
* ⭐ Rated

Over time, workers build a **portable professional identity**.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

```text
1. Worker completes a job
2. Sends QR / verification link
3. Client confirms in seconds
4. System logs the entry permanently
```

---

## 🔁 Real-World Flow

```text
Worker → Sends link (WhatsApp)
        ↓
Client → Opens → Confirms work
        ↓
System → Logs proof + payment + rating
```

---

## 🚀 Why This Matters

* 💳 Enables credit access (like a CIBIL for workers)
* 🧾 Builds verifiable work history
* 🛠 Helps workers charge premium rates
* 🤝 Creates instant trust with new clients

---

## 🧠 Core Insight

> People don’t lack work.
> They lack **proof of work**.
